About Us

This system helps farmers and breeders monitor livestock breeding in real-time, keep records, and maintain healthy animal management.

What Our System Offers

Breeding Monitoring

Track breeding schedules, mating history, and pregnancy records to ensure successful reproduction.

Health & Mortality Records

Record animal health conditions and monitor deceased animals for better analysis and prevention.

Accurate Data Management

Maintain organized records that help farmers make informed decisions and improve farm productivity.

Owner Information

This system is owned and managed by a livestock farm operator committed to improving animal care and farm efficiency through the use of modern technology.

The owner has over 10 years of experience in livestock management and has implemented modern digital solutions to streamline farm operations, ensuring healthier animals and increased productivity.

About the System

The LiveStock Breeding Monitoring System is designed for farmers, farm managers, and agricultural organizations who want a simple yet effective tool to manage livestock records and monitor breeding activities.
